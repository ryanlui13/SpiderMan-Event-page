Actor highlights

Toby


Toby was the first person who brought SpiderMan into real life. He portrayed the "nerdy" side of Peter Parker extremely well in the movies. From the actor's perspective, he was extremely grateful to collaborate with Andrew and Tom in "No Way Home". He's open to playing SpiderMan one more time. Will he be in SpiderMan 4? Only time will tell us!

Andrew

Andrew Garfield enjoys playing Spider-Man. Over time, he became extremely well-known for being THE Spiderman in our M.C.U. Most people only know him as the guy who plays SpiderMan, which is something he wants to change. He wants people to focus on the story being protrayed in the movies, rather than soley on him. Being Spiderman allowed him to have amazing opportunities he would not have got if he wasn't Spiderman. He had the chance to work with Matrin Scorses. Regardless, he is grateful for being able to play SpiderMan.

Tom

Tom Holland was at a conference in London, where someone asked him which superhero he would play? It's been his life-long dream to play Spider-Man. Little did he know, he became our new SpiderMan. To this day, he can't believe he got the role. When starting his role, one of the highlights he shares with the world was putting on the SpiderMan costume for "No Way Home"! Before "HomeComing": Tom Holland: He joked that he would be an undercover student in a high school in New York. He wanted to learn more about the life of an American.

Another crossover: Tom Holland confirms staring as Spiderman in the new Spiderman 4 movie that will come out soon. Will Andrew Garfield and Toby Maguire also be in the new movie? Who knows?!
